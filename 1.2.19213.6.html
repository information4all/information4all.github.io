Hi! I am lecturer at university of Bery.Here I a have some advice for students who face some difficulties in study.
Nowadays the syllabus keep changing( which means students mind should also change toward the subjects and their exams).In short, we can divide this subject-(PHYSICS) into two:

  1. Theory.
  2. Calculation.
.Here are some tips and hints that can guide you to obtain success in your exam.
  1. Theory

    2. When I a say theory I mean the laws,definitions,lab practicals and reasons behind the physics.For example
    • The Newton's law of motion.
    • What's the definition pressure?
      pressure is force per area unit.
    • Experiment such as:
      Brownian motion.
  2. Calculation

    3. I mean calculations to extract finall values from the values that you are given.For example:
    • How to find the pressure exerted by an object on a surface of something.
    • Finding the density of a matter.
